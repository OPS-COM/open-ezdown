Welcome to EZDown

The ultimate way to extract high-quality video and audio directly from your browser. Let's get you set up in just two steps.

Install Browser Extension

EZDown requires a companion Chrome extension to detect videos and pass them to this app.

  1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Turn on "Developer mode" (top right).
  3. Click "Load unpacked" (top left).
  4. Click the button below to easily find the folder, and select it!

Privacy & Terms

To bypass quality restrictions on sites like YouTube, EZDown uses your local session cookies to impersonate your browser.

Your data never leaves your computer. Cookies are sent directly from the extension to this desktop app via a local port, and used strictly for yt-dlp extraction.
